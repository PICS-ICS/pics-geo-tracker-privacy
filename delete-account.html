⚠ This action is permanent. Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile and associated data from PICS Geo Tracker. This cannot be undone.

How to request account deletion

To delete your PICS Geo Tracker account and all associated personal data, send a deletion request by email. Follow the steps below:

1
Compose an email to us

Send your request to info@ics.co.za using the email address linked to your PICS account.

2
Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – PICS Geo Tracker"

This ensures your request is routed correctly and processed without delay.

3
Include your details in the email body

Provide your full name, ID number or employee/contractor number, and the name of your employer or contracting company as registered on the PICS platform.

4
We will confirm and process your request

You will receive a confirmation email within 2 business days. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 5 business days of your request being verified.

Ready to submit your request? Tap the button below to open a pre-addressed email.

Send deletion request info@ics.co.za

What data is deleted and what is kept

Upon successful verification of your deletion request, the following action is taken on all data associated with your account:

Data type Action Details
Profile information (name, ID, contact details) Permanently deleted Removed within 5 business days of verified request
GPS location check-in / check-out records Permanently deleted All location history is purged from our systems
Attendance photos Permanently deleted All photos captured during check-ins are deleted
Login and usage logs Permanently deleted App session and event logs are removed
Device information Permanently deleted Device model and OS records are removed
All personal data associated with your PICS Geo Tracker account is permanently deleted within 5 business days. No personal data is retained after this period.

Important notes

  • Deleting your account from PICS Geo Tracker does not automatically remove your profile from the main PICS platform at pics.ics.co.za. Contact your employer or administrator to manage your platform profile separately.
  • Once your data is deleted it cannot be recovered. Please ensure you no longer require access before submitting a request.
  • If your employer requires you to use the App as part of your employment contract, please discuss your request with your employer before submitting.
  • We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request to protect against unauthorised deletion.
