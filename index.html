1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how LEMASPACE PTY LTD, trading as ICS Flow and Inspired Change Solutions ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use the PICS Geo Tracker mobile application ("the App").

PICS Geo Tracker is an employee and contractor management tool used exclusively by registered users of the PICS platform at pics.ics.co.za. It verifies that field personnel are present at their designated job sites at the required times.

By using this App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. This App is intended for use only by authorised employees and contractors of organisations registered on the PICS platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Category Examples Purpose
Account data Name, ID number, employer/contractor profile linked from pics.ics.co.za User identification and access control
Location data GPS coordinates, timestamp of check-in/check-out Verify on-site attendance at assigned job sites
Camera data Photos captured at check-in/check-out if required by your employer Visual confirmation of on-site presence
Device data Device model, OS version, app version Technical support and app compatibility
Usage logs Login times, check-in events, errors Audit trail and troubleshooting

We do not collect payment information, call logs, contacts, messages, or browsing history.

3. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions on your Android device:

  • Location (Precise / GPS): Required to determine whether you are physically present at your assigned job site. Location is captured only during active check-in/check-out events and is not tracked continuously in the background without your knowledge.
  • Camera: Used to capture a photo as visual confirmation of attendance, where required by your employer or job site configuration. Photos are not used for facial recognition.

You may deny or revoke these permissions in your device settings; however, doing so will prevent core features of the App from functioning.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To verify that employees and contractors are present at their designated job sites at the correct times.
  • To generate attendance records accessible to your employer or contracting company via the PICS platform.
  • To maintain an audit trail for compliance, payroll, and safety purposes.
  • To improve app performance and fix technical issues.
  • To communicate important updates related to the App or your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Sharing of Information

Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Your employer / contracting company: Attendance records, location check-ins, and photos are made available to the organisation that manages your profile on pics.ics.co.za.
  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party providers (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics) who are bound by data processing agreements and may not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information where required by South African law, a court order, or a regulatory authority.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

6. Data Retention

Location check-in records, attendance logs, and associated photos are retained for a minimum of 12 months from the date of capture, or for as long as required by your employer's contractual obligations and applicable South African labour and compliance law.

Account data is retained for as long as your profile remains active on the PICS platform. Upon account deactivation, personal data is deleted or anonymised within 90 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration. These include encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details in Section 11.

9. Children's Privacy

PICS Geo Tracker is intended for use by adults (18 years and older) in a professional employment context. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users via the App or email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes have been posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
